**Building Character**<br>
Building Character is the core of our mission and sets the foundation for programme development.<br>
All our pupils should stand tall in Character and all our staff demonstrate exemplary behaviour.

**Enriching Lives**<br>
Enriching Lives is about providing our school community with a holistic education and skills to face the challenges of the 21st Century.

**Stretching PoTential**<br>
Stretching Potential is about giving opportunities to all to excel to the best of their ability.
