<h3><strong>School Rules</strong></h3>
<p>1. Be neatly dressed in the correct school attire.</p>
<p>2. Be punctual for school.</p>
<p>3. Be courteous and considerate at all times.</p>
<p>4. Take proper care of school property.</p>
<p>5. Keep the school premises clean.</p>
<p>6. Queue up for food and drinks in the canteen.</p>
<p>7. Line up quietly during assembly and after recess.</p>
<p>8. Walk quietly and speak softly in the school premises.</p>
<p>9. Do not leave the school compound without permission during school hours.</p>
<p>10. Greet the teachers/staff or any visitors to the school.</p>
<p>11. Students are to wear the prescribed school uniform and modification
to the uniform is not allowed.</p>
<p>12. Students who are Singapore citizens must sing the National Anthem
and take the Pledge. Students will take the pledge with the right fist
over the heart.</p>
<p>13. Students must not keep a moustache or a beard.</p>
<p>14. All students are not allowed to have in their possession any weapon.
They are also not allowed to bring any weapon-like item which is used or
intended to be used to cause harm to others.</p>
