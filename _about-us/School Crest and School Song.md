<h3><strong>School Crest and School Song</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>School Crest</strong></h4>

</div>
<p>The school crest embodies the school's aims and aspirations.</p>
<p>The ‘P' signifies progress and prosperity.
<br>The ‘Y' signifies righteousness, steadfastness and strength.
<br>
</p>
<p>The colour red symbolises brotherhood. The colour white symbolises purity.
<br>Together, they represent mutual understanding and respect for one another.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The background colours of blue and white signify harmony and working towards
a bright and better future.</p>
