<p>Dear Parents/Guardians,</p>
<p>Teaching is a nurturing profession that shapes young minds and builds
foundations for children's futures. Various awards celebrate teachers who
show exceptional commitment and make significant educational impact. These
accolades honour educators who inspire students, develop creative teaching
methods and contribute to the broader educational community.</p>
<p>Do take time to appreciate our English/Malay/Tamil/Chinese Language teachers
and consider nominating them for these awards.</p>
