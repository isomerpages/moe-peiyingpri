<p>In alignment to CCE 2021, we aims to develop in our students:&nbsp;</p>
<p>a) <strong>Good character</strong>: Have a sound moral compass and a strong
sense of right and wrong, think critically and ethically, be discerning
in judgment, take responsibility for choices and actions, be caring towards
others and strive for excellence;&nbsp;</p>
<p>b) <strong>Resilience and social-emotional well-being</strong>: Have a
balanced sense of self, form healthy relationships, be resilient when faced
with challenges, find meaning in life, and have a sense of gratitude and
appreciation;&nbsp;</p>
<p>c) <strong>Future readiness</strong>: Have a sense of purpose in life,
develop the dispositions of adaptability and lifelong learning so as to
be able to navigate education and career pathways purposefully and take
on the challenges of the future, including the world of work and life;
and&nbsp;</p>
<p>d) <strong>Active citizenship</strong>: Develop a strong national identity
based on a sense of belonging to the nation, a sense of hope in themselves
and the future, an awareness of the reality of Singapore’s vulnerabilities
and constraints, and the will to act on improving the lives of others,
and building a future for our nation</p>

<h4>Values in Action (VIA)</h4>
<p>Our VIA programme aims to develop our students to be active contributors
and concerned citizens, fulfilling their responsibility to peers, school,
community and nation.</p>
<p>The school’s VIA programme focuses on two key areas; Act of kindness and
Environmental Conservation. The level P1 – 6 VIAs are designed to support
the learning and application of good values, knowledge and skills to attain
the CCE learning outcomes in a progressive manner.</p>

<h4>Student Recognition (i.e. PRIDE Star Award &amp; Catch Me Right Award)</h4>
<p>PRIDE Star Award &amp; Catch Me Right Award aims to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>encourage students to demonstrate core values consistently.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Recognise students for demonstrating the core values, showing good character
and for being a role model among their peers.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>In each term, students who have displayed exemplary conduct are nominated
by teachers and/ or peers for the award.&nbsp;</p>
<h4>Education and Career Guidance Experience</h4>
<p>At Peiying Primary School, Education and Career Guidance (ECG) initiatives
enhance students' learning experiences by fostering self-awareness, nurturing
career aspirations, and equipping them with lifelong skills through experiential
learning. These efforts aim to cultivate a growth mindset and resilience,
empowering students to embrace future opportunities with confidence. Through
meaningful engagement sessions, students gain a deeper appreciation for
the value of all occupations in society, build positive connections within
the community, and develop the ability to make informed decisions. They
are also encouraged to view setbacks as valuable opportunities for self-improvement
and personal growth, preparing them to navigate life's challenges with
optimism and perseverance.</p>
